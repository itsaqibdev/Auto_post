Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 4, 2024

1. Information We Collect

Our News Bot API collects and processes the following types of information:

  • API Credentials: Instagram access tokens and account IDs stored temporarily in memory or as environment variables
  • News Data: Public news articles fetched from third-party news APIs
  • Posted Content History: Record of news articles that have been posted to prevent duplicates
  • System Logs: Operational logs for debugging and monitoring purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used exclusively for:

  • Authenticating with Instagram/Facebook Graph API
  • Generating news card images from public news articles
  • Posting content to your Instagram Business Account
  • Preventing duplicate posts through history tracking
  • Maintaining and improving service functionality

3. Data Storage and Security

Important: This application is designed for personal use and runs in serverless mode on Vercel.

  • Environment Variables: Sensitive credentials are stored as environment variables in Vercel
  • No Database: We do not maintain a persistent database of user information
  • Session Data: Configuration changes are stored temporarily in memory during runtime
  • File Storage: Posted news history is stored in temporary JSON files (automatically cleared on redeployment)

4. Third-Party Services

This application integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Facebook Graph API: For posting content to Instagram Business Accounts
  • News API Services: For fetching public news articles
  • Vercel: For hosting and serverless deployment

Each service has its own privacy policy and terms of service that you should review.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law or to comply with legal processes
  • With service providers necessary for operation (Vercel, Instagram, News APIs)
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the configuration data stored in your deployment
  • Update or delete your API credentials at any time
  • Clear your posted news history
  • Revoke Instagram API access through Facebook Developer Console
  • Delete your entire deployment from Vercel

7. Instagram Data Policy Compliance

This application complies with Instagram's Platform Policy:

  • Only posts content to accounts you own and control
  • Does not collect or store Instagram user data beyond what's necessary
  • Respects Instagram's rate limits and posting guidelines
  • Uses official Instagram Graph API endpoints

8. Cookies and Tracking

This application does not use cookies or tracking technologies. The admin panel operates using session-based browser storage only.

9. Children's Privacy

This service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact Information

For questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact the application administrator or review the source code on GitHub.

Disclaimer: This is a self-hosted application. You are responsible for ensuring compliance with all applicable privacy laws and regulations in your jurisdiction.
